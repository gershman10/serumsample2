Dignity

  • January 07, 2020
  • Conversation

Dignity comes from accepting yourself as you are, precisely as you are, because you are.

There is no simpler, greater source of wealth. It is to accept yourself wholly and completely -- your limitations, weaknesses, and flaws; the pain you inflict, will inflict, and have inflicted; your hate, selfishness, and vanity; your constant propensity to fail, forget, and feign worthlessness; as well as your self-flagellation, self-rejection, and your repression of self. To accept and to forgive yourself, your whole self, is to calm all parts of yourself. It is to relax these parts that hurt and mar you. And the simple and sole justification of this acceptance is the mere reason that you are.

In the mirror you will find your worth. It is a worth radiates and emits from you. It is a worth that you cannot smear, cover, nor destroy. It is a worth that you can only amplify and grow. You are. The immaculate inner beauty that rests within you radiates outward when you allow it to pass through this gateway unhindered. Stand noble and tall. Do justice to what you are. There is nothing else that will truly convince you of your noble worth; no amount of recognition, no assignment of status, no mountain climbed nor ocean crossed. The only audience that can dignify you is the one that knows you best. It is in accepting yourself that you will finally feel accepted.
