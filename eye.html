The Eye

  • July 10, 2019
  • Stories


I am the Eye

I open

When I see others, they see me

When they see me, they see themselves.

For I am the Eye

In me there is clarity

When I open, they become open

And when they become open, they become themselves

And I see them.

I am a mirror.

Seeing me seeing them

Seeing me seeing them

And things become clear.

And eventually they learn.

Eventually they learn

That it's okay.
